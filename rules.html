Natan Rules

  • 1. Treat everyone with respect: Do not insult, abuse, or threaten other users, off-site or on-site.
  • 2. Be safe: Keep personal and contact information private. Do not share sensitive information such as your physical address. We cannot moderate behavior outside of the website.
  • 3. Be honest: Do not impersonate others or intentionally spread disinformation. Parody accounts should be marked as such.
  • 4. Try not to break the site: If you find any security issues, please stop before causing any damage and report issues to us.
  • 5. Have fun: Enjoy your time on Natan; please try to keep it a fun and safe place for everyone.
